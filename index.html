Getting to know the town outside of the university!

The Community

The above picture features the Blacksburg Farmers Market. A place which encourages you to not only be a local but to also buy local! They even offer an Antique Market! You can check out their website here - Blacksburg Farmers Market.

The scenery

Here we see this adorable pup enjoying her hike to The Cascades! Blacksburg and the surrounding area are filled with outdoor opportunities. The website The Hiking Hokie offers a multitude of hiking options!

The food

Pictured is one of the famous Jumbo Margaritas from El Rods, a great pairing to go with their Mexican style dishes! Blacksburg is filled with delicious food and drink opportunities. Don't believe me? Just check out this A-Z list on BlacksburgMenu.com
